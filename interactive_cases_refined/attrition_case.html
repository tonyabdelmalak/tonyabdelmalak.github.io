Problem & Context

Early attrition can be costly and disruptive. Leaders often lack insight into which new hires are at risk and why. This dashboard helps explore key drivers of attrition so you can identify intervention opportunities.

Approach & Visualizations

The scatter plot shows predicted attrition risk by overtime status and job satisfaction (synthetic data). Marker size corresponds to monthly income. Use the chart to identify clusters of high‑risk employees. The bar chart ranks the importance of top features from a logistic regression model.

Overtime indicates whether an employee works overtime. Marker size represents monthly income.
Importance values are derived from a logistic regression model (absolute coefficients).

Key Insights & Recommendations

Employees with high overtime and low job satisfaction have the highest attrition risk. Prioritizing work‑life balance initiatives and boosting job satisfaction through improved onboarding, manager training, and feedback programs can reduce early attrition.
