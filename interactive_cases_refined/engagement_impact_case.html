Interactive Case Study: Measure Engagement Impact

Explore how employee engagement relates to productivity and turnover across departments. Use the dropdown to filter by department and view interactive charts.

*Point size reflects tenure (years at company). Departments are color-coded. Use the dropdown to filter by department.
*Turnover rate is calculated as the proportion of employees who left during each month. Month index corresponds to the order of months in the dataset.
Key Insight: In Marketing, engagement and productivity have a correlation of 0.07, indicating that increasing engagement could lead to noticeable productivity gains.
