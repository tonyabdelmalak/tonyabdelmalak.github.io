Interactive Case Study: Recruitment Funnel Analytics

This dashboard demonstrates how candidates move through the recruitment funnel. The bar chart shows the number of candidates at each stage, and the line chart displays the conversion rate between stages.

Candidate Counts by Stage

*Stages include Application, Phone Screen, Interview, Offer and Hire.

Key insight: Most drop‑off occurs between the interview and offer stages. Streamlining interviews could improve conversion by 8 %.

Conversion Rate by Stage

*Conversion rate is the percentage of candidates who progress to the next stage.
