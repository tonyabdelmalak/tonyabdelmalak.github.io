About Me

I started in HR, but my curiosity for solving problems led me into analytics. Now I build dashboards, predictive models, and AI tools that help leaders make more informed decisions about their people and business.

I believe in humanizing data — using numbers to tell a story and drive actions that matter. Whether it’s optimizing workforce plans or predicting trends, I keep the focus on impact.

Outside of work, I’m often exploring the latest in tech, mentoring those pivoting into analytics, or fine-tuning my next AI project. I’m excited to share my journey and insights with you here!
