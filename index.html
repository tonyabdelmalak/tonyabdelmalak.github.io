About Me

People First, Data Second: I believe data is about people. My mission is to transform workforce analytics into human stories that empower decision-makers to build fair, sustainable workplaces.

I turn workforce and business data into AI-powered, decision-ready insights. After leading HR operations for major brands and startups, I pivoted into AI-driven analytics—combining people-strategy expertise with Tableau, SQL, Python and AI agents.

I design predictive models, interactive dashboards and AI assistants that translate complex datasets into clear, actionable strategies for executives. I’m based in Los Angeles and excited to connect.
